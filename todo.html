Visit the Chapel of Panagia Krimniotissa


There are about 900+ churches and chapels on this island but Krimniotissa is probably the most famous one of them all. Panagia refers to the Virgin Mary to whom the church is dedicated to. Exploring the chapel is free and great for kids and adults.


SANTORINI, GREECE



Santorini is one of the most remarkable beaches in the Aegean Sea, not because of its iconic black sands. A result of the island's volcanic heritage, they make quite a surprising sight. There is a diversity of colors and the landscapes they create; white, red and mostly black sand and pebbles adorn the beaches in Santorini. The dissimilar beaches all along the island will please everybody. The best part about this beach is that it is free of cost and a great beach day for the family!
